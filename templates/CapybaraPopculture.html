Capybara in Popculture

The Capybara's chill demeanor has caused it to have quite a rise in "meme" popculture in recent years, as demonstrated by the entertainment site Urban Dictionary.

Capybara has over 20 definitions and 300 upvotes on urbandictionary.com.

As to what the capybara thinks of its rise to fame, it's hard to tell from its stoic facial expression.

Top Capybara "memes" from Urban Dictionary include:

  • "ok i pull up", in reference to the capybara's connection to the song After Party by Don Toliver, representing the capybara's nonchalant nature.
  • Capybara - "Someone who is chill with everyone."
  • Capybara - "friend shaped".
