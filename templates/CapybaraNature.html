Capybara in Nature

The Capybara is one cute fellow. Due to this creature's cuteness, it may be tempting to over look its magnificent presence in nature.

Scuttle on over to the interesting capybara facts listed below, taken from Britannica Online Encyclopedia to see what this fellow looks like in nature.

Capybara Facts

  • Capybara are also known as carpincho or water hog.
  • Larger capybara are the largest rodent in the world.
  • Capybara are shy, but live in groups.
  • Capybara can dive into water to escape dangerous predators.
  • Some countries eat capybara meat.
